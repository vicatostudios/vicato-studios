About Vicato

Vicato is more than a fashion brand; it is a manifestation of self-expression, authenticity, and personal evolution. Rooted in creativity and individuality, Vicato represents the fusion of influences, inspirations, and the unique essence of its creator. Every piece reflects a deep connection to identity—a fearless embrace of one’s true self—and the aspiration to reach one’s highest potential.

At its core, Vicato encourages boldness: the courage to live authentically, pursue dreams without hesitation, and align with one’s ultimate purpose. It promotes a mindset of confidence, awareness, and flow, inspiring individuals to embody:

  • Love: A deep connection with oneself and the universe.
  • Inner Peace: A state of calmness, relaxation, and tranquility.
  • Confidence & Charisma: Embracing one’s individuality with charm and self-assurance.
  • Focus & Awareness: Staying present and driven toward personal goals.
  • Happiness & Depth: Balancing joy with introspection and purpose.

Vicato is an invitation to express yourself unapologetically and take ownership of your journey. It is a celebration of identity, creativity, and the relentless pursuit of personal and artistic truth.

Life Is a Maximalist Place

The world itself is a masterpiece of maximalism—a rich tapestry woven with intricate details, layers of meaning, and endless stories waiting to be discovered. Life is not minimalist; it is abundant, complex, and filled with moments of depth and significance. Every place, every interaction, and every creation carries an imprint of time, effort, and emotion, forming an ever-evolving museum of art that surrounds us.

Vicato embraces this philosophy, translating it into clothing that is more than just fabric—it is an artifact of expression. A Vicato piece is crafted with intention, layered with details that tell a story beyond aesthetics. It is not just about the artistic vision but also about the journey of its creation, the energy poured into every stitch, and the history embedded in each material. Every garment is a grail, a singular work of art that holds experiences, narratives, and significance beyond what meets the eye.

Maximalism is not just a style; it is a way of seeing the world. It is an appreciation for depth, intricacy, and the stories hidden within the details. A Vicato piece reflects this essence—bold, unique, and filled with meaning—because life itself is a maximalist place.

About the Creator

I am Carlos Viaud, a creator, visionary, and storyteller. Born in Costa Rica to a diverse and culturally rich family, my identity is shaped by a mix of backgrounds, experiences, and influences that have guided me toward my purpose. From an early age, I felt different from my peers—not in a way that distanced me but in a way that made me realize I see the world through a unique lens. I embrace my individuality, and it fuels my ambition. My passion is self-expression, and my purpose is to create.

I am deeply connected to art in all its forms. Fashion and music are my main creative outlets, but I see life itself as an art form—a canvas where every experience, every decision, and every detail contributes to a greater masterpiece. I believe in pushing boundaries, crafting stories, and leaving behind something meaningful. My journey began with a love for basketball, evolved into an appreciation for music, and ultimately led me to fashion, where I found my true calling.

Vicato is more than a brand; it is an extension of myself. Every piece carries history, craftsmanship, and a narrative. I believe in maximalism—not just as a design choice but as a philosophy. Life is intricate, layered, and rich with detail, and I bring that same essence into my creations. A Vicato piece is not just clothing; it is a work of art, a statement, and a reflection of the experiences that shaped it.

My approach to life is strategic and intentional. I believe in breaking down dreams into actionable steps, analyzing the path to success, and executing with precision. Manifestation is not just about believing in something; it is about understanding the journey and making it happen. I am constantly refining this skill, ensuring that every move I make aligns with my ultimate vision.

My influences come from many places—artists, designers, musicians, and visionaries who have left their mark on the world. Yet, my path is unique; there is no direct blueprint for what I aspire to achieve. I take inspiration from various sources, but I am carving my own way, blending creativity, business, and craftsmanship into something entirely new.

My daily routine is a reflection of my dedication. Mornings start with gratitude, fitness, and preparation for the work ahead. Creating Vicato pieces is a meticulous process—fading, distressing, layering, and stitching each pair of jeans with purpose. Every technique I use has been developed through experimentation and an obsession with detail. Sourcing materials, designing silhouettes, and executing each vision with precision are essential parts of my craft. Collaboration is also key—while I am increasingly involved in every step of production, I work with skilled artisans to bring my designs to life.

Beyond work, balance is essential. Life is about duality—the fast-paced excitement of ambition and the deep calm of self-awareness. I strive for both. There are days of intense focus and productivity, and there are moments of celebration, connection, and living in the present. I nourish my body and mind, yet I also embrace the imperfections and contradictions that make life real. I am here to experience everything fully, without fear, without regret—just a commitment to being my true self.

Vicato is my dream, my legacy, and my way of sharing my perspective with the world. It is a tribute to the figures, experiences, and ideas that have shaped me. It is about maximizing potential, reaching for something greater, and creating a reality that aligns with the highest version of myself. This is just the beginning.
